    This high school workshop will explore the pros, cons, controversies, and utility of ChatGPT and other AI tools. We will discuss the potential for academic dishonesty, as well as ethical issues surrounding the use of AI. Additionally, we will examine common and practical uses of ChatGPT, such as language generation and machine learning. We will also delve into the history of AI, tracing its development and impact on various industries. Finally, we will discuss the implications of AI for the future of work and education, considering the potential for automation and the impact on job markets. Overall, this workshop aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of the current state and future possibilities of AI technology.
